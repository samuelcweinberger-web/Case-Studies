# 04. Benchmarking, Responsible Gaming & Research at Scale

**Company:** FanDuel  
**Context:** FanDuel · Core Products & Experiences  
**Year:** 2021–2025

**Listing blurb:** Scaled UX research 2→32 and built regulator-approved RG measurement—while cutting research turnaround 75%.

## Summary
Built continuous cross-product benchmarking, scaled UX research 2→32 people (200+ studies/year), and delivered a regulator-approved Responsible Gaming Sentiment Scale—cutting research turnaround 75%.

> Measure every product the same way and the roadmap starts finding you—roughly 60% of my FanDuel projects began as benchmarking findings.

## Impact KPIs
- **2 → 32** — UX researchers in the division  _[scale]_
- **200+** — Studies delivered annually  _[scale]_
- **75% reduction** — Research turnaround time (AI-enabled pipelines)  _[efficiency]_
- **1.5M → 20M+** — Users across multi-state rollouts  _[scale]_

## Situation
- FanDuel’s products were fragmented across five apps and regions with no shared UX baseline.
- The research team was two people heading into multi-state expansion.

## Task
- Stand up standardized benchmarking across products and competitors.
- Scale research capacity and build Responsible Gaming measurement fit for Legal/Compliance and regulators.

## Action
- Built continuous journey benchmarking with event-triggered surveys feeding stakeholder dashboards.
- Developed and psychometrically validated a proprietary RG Sentiment Scale tied to compliance signals.
- Scaled ops and AI research pipelines as hiring grew the org.

## Result
- Grew UX Research from 2 to a 32-person division delivering 200+ studies/year; cut turnaround 75%.
- Supported multi-state growth from 1.5M to 20M+ users.
- RG Sentiment Scale earned regulatory approval for predictive user-protection models.
